# AI4I-Contribute Overview

AI4I-Contribute is the Data Ingestion and Annotation building block within the AI4Inclusion ecosystem.

It enables large-scale creation of high-quality datasets across:

* Speech
* Text
* Images
* Video
* Multimodal combinations

Contribute integrates with dataset registries, quality validation modules, training orchestrators, and governance systems to ensure interoperability across the Language AI lifecycle.
