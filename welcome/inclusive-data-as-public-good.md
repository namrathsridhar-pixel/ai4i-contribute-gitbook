# Inclusive Data as a Public Good

AI4I-Contribute is offered as a Digital Public Good (DPG).

As a DPG, Contribute:

* Uses open standards
* Enables cross-platform interoperability
* Reduces duplication of effort across institutions
* Lowers the cost of dataset creation
* Supports global adoption and localization

By treating multilingual data as public digital infrastructure, Contribute enables countries to preserve linguistic diversity, empower citizens, and accelerate AI innovation responsibly.
